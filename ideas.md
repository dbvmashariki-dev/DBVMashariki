## Approach 1: Corporate Gravitas
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Neo-Corporate Modernism  
**Core Principles:** Authority through restraint; data-driven visual hierarchy; structured whitespace as a power signal; monochromatic depth with a single accent.  
**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0A1628) backgrounds with warm off-white (#F5F0E8) text. A single gold accent (#C9A84C) used sparingly for CTAs and highlights — communicating premium value without ostentation.

**Animation:** Slow, confident entrance animations (0.8s ease-out). Text lines reveal from bottom. Cards lift 4px on hover with a shadow expansion.  
**Typography System:** Display: Playfair Display (bold, italic for emphasis). Body: Source Sans Pro (regular 400, medium 500). Hierarchy: 72px hero → 40px section → 24px card → 16px body.

<response>
<text>

**Core Principles:** Grid discipline with intentional breaks; typography as the primary visual element; motion that communicates momentum; clarity over decoration.  
**Color Philosophy:** Slate-charcoal (#1C2B3A) as the dominant dark tone. Crisp white (#FFFFFF) for content areas. Electric teal (#00B4D8) as the action/accent color — forward-looking, technology-adjacent, trustworthy. Warm gray (#F4F6F8) for alternating section backgrounds.  
**Layout Paradigm:** Strict 12-column grid with deliberate full-bleed breaks. Hero uses a large typographic statement left-aligned with a right-side image panel. Service cards use a horizontal scroll-snap on mobile and a 2×2 asymmetric grid on desktop.

**Interaction Philosophy:** Purposeful micro-interactions. Navigation links underline from center. Service cards reveal a teal border on hover. CTA buttons have a fill-sweep animation.  
**Animation:** Framer Motion scroll-triggered reveals. Staggered card entrance (0.1s delay between items). Hero text splits by word with a 0.05s stagger.  
**Typography System:** Display: DM Sans (800 weight). Body: DM Sans (400/500). Accent labels: DM Mono (uppercase, tracked). Scale: 80px hero → 48px h2 → 28px h3 → 17px body.

## Approach 3: Organic Authority
<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Warm Minimalism / Editorial Consulting  
**Core Principles:** Human-centered warmth within a professional shell; editorial pacing with generous whitespace; photography as the primary storytelling medium; color as emotional cue.  
**Color Philosophy:** Warm cream (#FAF7F2) base. Deep forest green (#1A3C34) for primary text and headers — grounded, trustworthy, growth-oriented. Terracotta (#C4622D) as a secondary accent for energy and urgency. Stone gray (#8C8C8C) for supporting text.  
**Layout Paradigm:** Magazine-style editorial layout. Full-bleed photography sections. Pull quotes as large typographic features. Staggered card grid with varying heights (masonry-inspired).  
**Signature Elements:** Large circular image crops; hand-drawn underline SVG accents; generous paragraph leading (1.9) for a considered, thoughtful reading experience.  
**Interaction Philosophy:** Tactile and warm. Images scale gently on hover (1.03). Smooth page transitions. Navigation uses a full-screen overlay menu.  
**Animation:** Gentle, organic motion. Images parallax at 0.3× scroll speed. Section headers write in character by character. Cards fade up with a slight rotation (2deg → 0deg).  
**Typography System:** Display: Cormorant Garamond (700 italic). Body: Lato (400). Labels: Lato (700 uppercase). Scale: 76px hero → 44px h2 → 26px h3 → 16px body.
</text>
